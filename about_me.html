About Me

Howdy!

My name is John Plunk and I am a student at Texas A&M University. I am a student in the Electrical And Computer Engineering department, and I am a Computer Engineering major. I am also planning on receiving a minor in both Cybersecurity and Math! I am also a student tech in the Biology Department, working for their IT division with two sysadmins. I'm also an Eagle Scout!

I am from a small town up in Fort Worth, Texas called Aledo. Born in 1999 to be the youngest of three children, I have always been in interested in STEM fields. As a young Cub Scout, my favorite part of the year was when we would hold the Pinewood Derby races. My dad and I would hop the fence to secretly use my neighbors workshop to create a wooden car for the races.

In high school, I graduated in 2017 in the top 10% of my class. I was an officer of the Tri-M Music Honor Society and a member of the NHS. I was also in marching band, concert band, and jazz band! I exceled in my Computer Science classes, and I was able to achieve the Eagle Scout rank in my senior year of high school.

These days, you'll find me here at Texas A&M studying my heart out in my STEM classes. When I'm not studying and going to class, I attend IEEE events (and hackathons!) and help front the service organization Students Serving Scouting as vice-president. When I'm not backpacking or camping on weekends, you'll find me watching bad TV shows and movies with my roommates (our guilty pleasure), or playing tennis with friends!
